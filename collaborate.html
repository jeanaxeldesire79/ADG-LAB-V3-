Careers & Collaboration Opportunities

We welcome consultants, researchers, and partners who want to build data-driven solutions with us.

Current focus areas

  • • Practice leads and delivery strategists for embedded engagements
  • • Independent contributors spanning data, policy, design, and storytelling
  • • Builders of data platforms, digital products, and insight infrastructure
  • • Research fellows and residency designers exploring new evidence

What to include

  • • One-page dossier or CV highlighting mission-driven work
  • • Short note outlining the pathway you’re exploring and availability
  • • Evidence of craft (case study, data product, publication, or prototype)
  • • Optional references, testimonials, or collaborator endorsements

Send materials to our people team: hr@axeldevlab.com

We review submissions on a rolling basis and will reach out when there is a strong match.
