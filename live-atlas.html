Strategic Overview Public Value

Continental Perspective, Local Precision

Live Atlas delivers a unified, visual language for Africa’s development story. Nations, regions, and multilateral partners can see how infrastructure, climate, and economic systems are shifting in real time.

The platform bridges top-level diplomacy with grounded local realities. Dashboards scale from continental narratives to city-level detail without losing the context decision-makers require.

What Live Atlas Enables

  • Geospatial dashboards that surface economic, climate, and social indicators in a single pane.
  • Scenario canvases for policy, investment, and cooperation briefs.
  • Storytelling modules that translate complex data into clear, shareable narratives.
Core Features

A Living Map for Strategic Decisions

From national dashboards to regional cooperation portals, Live Atlas offers modular features that keep institutions informed and aligned.

Continental & National Dashboards

Layered views of infrastructure, governance, and development indicators with drill-down to local projects.

Real-Time Mapping

Dynamic layers for economic, climate, and social indicators updated with trusted data feeds.

Interactive Storytelling

Curated journeys that transform data into narratives for cabinet briefings, donor updates, and citizen engagement.

Impact & Use Cases

Turning Complex Data into Shared Insight

Live Atlas is built for decision rooms, diplomatic missions, and public platforms that need to see the whole picture.

  • Pan-African cooperation briefings with geo-contextual intelligence.
  • National infrastructure planning powered by layered insights from energy to logistics.
  • Storytelling microsites that help citizens and partners understand development trajectories.

Why leaders depend on Live Atlas

  • Integrates seamlessly with Axel Stack and ADIM for trusted data pipelines.
  • Offers publish-ready exports for policy briefs, donor decks, and press releases.
  • Supports multilingual, multi-tenant deployments across the continent.
