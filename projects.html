Live Atlas — Mapping Africa’s Development Systems

Live Atlas is a living, interactive map of Africa’s development, governance, and infrastructure systems, helping institutions see what is changing in real time.

Building Africa’s Data Backbone.

ADIM — African Data Infrastructure Mapping (PoC)

ADIM demonstrates Axel Dev Group’s vision for a connected, data-sovereign Africa, integrating economic, demographic, and environmental data into a continental prototype.

Strategic Overview

Showcases a blueprint for African-built infrastructure where national datasets and global systems interoperate without losing sovereignty.

Core Features

  • Multi-sectoral harmonization pilot connecting national statistical offices.
  • Fusion of national and global datasets for resilient planning.
  • Open visualization prototype designed for institutions and the public.
Public Impact

Demonstrates Africa’s ability to own, govern, and visualize its data systems — a cornerstone of digital independence.
