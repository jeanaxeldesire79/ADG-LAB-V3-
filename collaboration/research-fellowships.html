Research Fellowships

Partner with Axel Dev Lab to advance evidence that shifts policy and unlocks equitable systems. We collaborate with researchers, civic technologists, and storytellers who push beyond conventional outputs.

Fellowship cohort

Advance evidence that shapes narratives and decisions.

Our fellowships convene researchers, policy entrepreneurs, and creative technologists to generate deep narratives, longitudinal data stories, and actionable playbooks for governments and ecosystems in transition.

Submit fellowship concept Review collaboration menu

Fellowship span

6–12 months

Part-time with structured sprint cycles

Focus pillars

Governance · Climate · Inclusive growth

Plus civic tech & future of work

What we expect

Elevate evidence into movements and mandates.

Fellows lead flagship research, foresight, and storytelling programs with our ecosystem partners. We seek scholars and practitioners who combine intellectual rigor with a systems and justice lens.

Rigorous inquiry

Design research architectures, triangulate data sources, and uphold ethical standards while interrogating power, context, and narrative.

Collaborative authorship

Co-develop knowledge products with communities, practitioners, and creatives—ensuring shared authorship and contextual resonance.

Policy fluency

Translate insights into actionable recommendations, investment pipelines, and engagement strategies for decision-makers.

How to become one

A fellowship design sprint to ensure alignment and depth.

We co-create each fellowship around your research agenda and the communities it serves. Expect intentional conversations that explore scope, governance, and resourcing.

Step 01

Submit research agenda

Share your fellowship thesis, geographic anchors, collaborators, and anticipated outputs. We assess resonance with our impact roadmap and partner demand.

Step 02

Co-design study architecture

Work with our practice leads and community advisors to refine methods, governance, open-data plans, and storytelling formats.

Step 03

Launch with editorial & policy support

Finalise partnership agreements, set milestones, and assemble an editorial + policy translation squad to amplify findings as they emerge.

What you gain

Design research inside a platform built for influence.

Publication platform

Co-publish longform reports, multimedia narratives, datasets, and policy briefs with distribution across our institutional network.

Data infrastructure

Access proprietary data pipelines, GIS assets, modeling support, and research assistants to operationalise your methodology.

Policy convenings

Present at closed-door briefings, multilateral roundtables, and public salons curated by Axel Dev Lab to move ideas into action.

Materials to submit

Assemble a fellowship dossier that signals depth and vision.

We welcome nuance. Include links where helpful and specify embargoed or confidential sections—we will protect sensitive information.

  • • Curriculum vitae highlighting research outputs, institutional affiliations, and language capabilities
  • • Fellowship concept note (2–3 pages) outlining the core question, context, methods, and timeline
  • • Representative writing sample or creative research artefact (published or unpublished)
  • • Two references or letters of support (academic, community, or practitioner)

Send materials to

hr@axeldevlab.com

Subject line: “Research Fellowship – [Theme]”.

Responses within 15 business days. Shortlisted fellows join a design colloquium.
