How to become one

A streamlined process built around speed and transparency.

We minimise paperwork and maximise clarity. Expect structured touchpoints that surface your strengths, align expectations, and accelerate onboarding.

Step 01

Share your capabilities deck

Send your CV, rate card, recent engagements, and preferred collaboration model. We tag you to live or upcoming opportunities within five business days.

Step 02

Alignment sprints

Join a 45-minute working session with the practice lead to walk through a micro-brief. We assess fit, clarify scope preferences, and map out collaboration rituals.

Step 03

Onboarding & first assignment

Finalise the master services agreement, access our knowledge base, and kick off with a buddy from our delivery team for your first project.

Materials to submit

Curate a capsule that showcases breadth and depth.

  • • Rate card and billing preferences, including currencies and time zone
