Research Protocols

  • • Sourcing & Licensing: prioritize official/peer-reviewed sources; respect licenses.
  • • Ethics & Privacy: IRB where applicable, consent, de-identification, DUAs.
  • • Reproducible Pipelines: version control, notebooks, environment lockfiles.
  • • Validation & QA: automated checks, peer review, anomaly investigations.

Governance & Standards

  • • Documentation: method cards, data dictionaries, change logs.
  • • Versioning: semantic releases with provenance and rollback plans.
  • • Security: least-privilege access, encryption in transit/at rest.
  • • Transparency: public summaries; internal detailed annexes on request.

Quality & Governance

Accountable evidence, governed end-to-end.

Our governance framework ensures every deliverable is reviewable, reproducible, and traceable. Responsibilities sit with Data Stewardship, Methodology Council, and Client Delivery teams.

Release Cadence

  • • Quarterly: energy maps, macro dashboards, governance reports
  • • Bi-annual: demographic datasets, youth employment brief
  • • Rolling: humanitarian scans, client-specific scorecards

Roles & accountability

Every pipeline has a data steward, methodology lead, and reviewer. Approvals recorded in Notion control room and synced to Git history.

Documentation standards

Method cards stored as Markdown with version numbers, change log, and risk notes. Rendered to HTML/PDF for client delivery.

Quality scorecard

Each release graded across freshness, completeness, accuracy, bias checks, and documentation depth. Scores shared with clients.

Resource center

Download briefs, data dictionaries, and QA checklists.

PDF summaries are kept in sync with the internal Notion space. Request access for restricted or client-specific documentation.
